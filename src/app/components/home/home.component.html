Welcome To JICDS’20

International Conference on Data Science 2020 JICDS’20 is a second International conference, for sharing knowledge about cutting edge research and applications of the multidisciplinary field of data science. The conference will bring together researchers and practitioners from academia and industry to exchange their knowledge and discuss challenges and goals for data science.

The conference will be held on February 7-9, 2021 at Mohammad Ali Jinnah University, Karachi.

The program of the conference will consist of paper presentations, poster presentations, system exhibition, doctoral colloquium, invited talks and panel discussions.
