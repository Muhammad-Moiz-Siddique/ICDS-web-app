Jinnah International Conference on Data Science 2021 (JICDS'21), is a second international conference for sharing knowledge about cutting edge research and applications of the multidisciplinary field of data science. The conference will bring together researchers and practitioners from academia and industry to exchange their knowledge and discuss about challenges and goals for data science.

The conference will be organized on February 9-10, 2021 by Mohammad Ali Jinnah University, Karachi. Due to precautions for the second wave of Covid19 pandemic, the conference will be held online.
